### CSC 110 Final Application: Mapping Life Expectancy in North Carolina

This R Shiny app maps life expectancy across North Carolina counties, correlating it with health and demographic factors such as income, education, healthcare access, and race. It offers an interactive way to explore these relationships.

### Key Features
- Interactive Map: Visualize life expectancy by county.
- Overlay Factors: Compare life expectancy with factors like income, education, healthcare access, and race.
- Customizable Views: Adjust the map to explore different factors.
